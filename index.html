About me

I hold an M.Sc. in Applied Artificial Intelligence and Data Analytics from the University of Bradford, with a background in Electrical and Electronic Engineering. My work focuses on applying artificial intelligence and data-driven methodologies to solve complex, real-world problems. I am particularly interested in machine learning, intelligent data systems, and the practical deployment of AI in impactful domains. I have experience analyzing real-world datasets, developing predictive models, and transforming data into actionable insights to support decision-making.

My long-term goal is to advance applied AI research, with a strong interest in healthcare analytics, including AI-driven diagnosis, prognosis, and intelligent clinical decision support systems, along with broader applications in automation and intelligent systems.
